## 2. Patient Portal

### 2.1 Account Registration

1. Visit your clinic's patient portal URL
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your personal information
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile with emergency contacts

### 2.2 Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your personal dashboard with:

- **Next Appointment**: Upcoming visit details
- **Recent Medications**: Current prescriptions
- **Lab Results**: Recent test results
- **Messages**: Communications from your healthcare team

### 2.3 Appointment Management

#### Booking an Appointment
1. Click **"Schedule Appointment"** from your dashboard
2. Select your preferred provider or service
3. Choose an available date and time
4. Enter the reason for your visit
5. Confirm your booking

#### Rescheduling Appointments
1. Go to **"My Appointments"**
2. Find the appointment you want to change
3. Click **"Reschedule"**
4. Select a new date and time
5. Confirm the change

#### Canceling Appointments
1. Go to **"My Appointments"**
2. Find the appointment to cancel
3. Click **"Cancel"**
4. Select a reason for cancellation
5. Confirm cancellation

### 2.4 Medical Records Access

#### Viewing Medical History
1. Click **"Medical Records"** from the menu
2. Select the type of records you want to view:
   - Visit History
   - Medications
   - Lab Results
   - Immunizations
   - Allergies

#### Downloading Records
1. Open the desired medical record
2. Click **"Download"** or **"Print"**
3. Choose your preferred format (PDF, etc.)
4. Save or print the document

### 2.5 Prescription Management

#### Viewing Prescriptions
1. Click **"Medications"** from your dashboard
2. View your active prescriptions
3. Check refill status and instructions

#### Requesting Refills
1. Find the medication that needs refilling
2. Click **"Request Refill"**
3. Confirm the request
4. You'll receive a notification when it's ready

### 2.6 Communication

#### Sending Messages
1. Click **"Messages"** from the menu
2. Click **"New Message"**
3. Select the recipient (your doctor, nurse, etc.)
4. Enter your message subject and content
5. Click **"Send"**

#### Viewing Messages
1. Go to **"Messages"**
2. Click on any message to read it
3. Reply directly or start a new conversation

### 2.7 Billing and Payments

#### Viewing Bills
1. Click **"Billing"** from the menu
2. View your account balance and recent charges
3. Review itemized bills

#### Making Payments
1. Click **"Pay Bill"** on any outstanding balance
2. Enter your payment information
3. Confirm the payment amount
4. Complete the secure payment process

## 3. Receptionist Guide

### 3.1 Daily Workflow

#### Morning Setup
1. Log in to the system
2. Review today's appointments
3. Check for any urgent messages or alerts
4. Prepare the front desk area

#### Patient Check-in Process
1. Greet the patient and verify their identity
2. Search for the patient in the system
3. Confirm their appointment details
4. Update insurance information if needed
5. Collect any co-payments
6. Mark the patient as checked in
7. Direct them to the waiting area

### 3.2 Patient Registration

#### New Patient Registration
1. Click **"Patients"** > **"New Patient"**
2. Enter personal information:
   - Full name and date of birth
   - Contact information
   - Emergency contacts
   - Insurance details
3. Add medical history if available
4. Save the patient record
5. Print patient labels or wristbands if needed

#### Walk-in Patient Registration
1. Click **"Quick Registration"**
2. Enter minimal required information
3. Create a temporary appointment
4. Complete full registration later

### 3.3 Appointment Management

#### Scheduling Appointments
1. Click **"Appointments"** > **"New Appointment"**
2. Search for and select the patient
3. Choose the provider and service type
4. Select an available time slot
5. Enter appointment details and notes
6. Send confirmation to the patient

#### Managing the Daily Schedule
1. View the **"Today's Schedule"**
2. Monitor wait times and room utilization
3. Handle no-shows and cancellations
4. Reschedule appointments as needed
5. Coordinate with clinical staff

## 4. Nurse Guide

### 4.1 Daily Routine

#### Shift Start
1. Log in and review your assignments
2. Check for pending vital signs or tasks
3. Review patient list and priorities
4. Prepare your workstation

#### Patient Preparation
1. Call patient from waiting room
2. Escort to examination room
3. Record vital signs (blood pressure, temperature, etc.)
4. Update patient allergies and medications
5. Prepare patient for doctor visit
6. Document nursing notes

### 4.2 Vital Signs Recording

#### Recording Vitals
1. Select the patient from your assignment list
2. Click **"Record Vitals"**
3. Enter measurements:
   - Blood Pressure (systolic/diastolic)
   - Heart Rate
   - Respiratory Rate
   - Temperature
   - Oxygen Saturation
   - Weight and Height (BMI calculated automatically)
   - Pain Scale (0-10)
4. Add any observations or notes
5. Save and notify the doctor

#### Vital Signs Trends
1. View historical vital signs
2. Identify trends or abnormalities
3. Flag critical values for immediate attention
4. Document concerns in patient notes

### 4.3 Patient Assessment

#### Pre-Visit Assessment
1. Review patient's medical history
2. Verify allergies and current medications
3. Assess chief complaint and symptoms
4. Perform basic physical assessment
5. Document findings and concerns

#### Triage and Prioritization
1. Assess patient condition upon arrival
2. Assign appropriate priority level
3. Flag urgent cases for immediate attention
4. Coordinate with physicians for critical patients

### 4.4 Medication Administration

#### Medication Verification
1. Review ordered medications
2. Check for allergies and interactions
3. Verify "Five Rights" of medication administration:
   - Right patient
   - Right medication
   - Right dose
   - Right route
   - Right time
4. Document administration

#### Patient Education
1. Explain medication purpose and instructions
2. Demonstrate proper administration if needed
3. Provide written instructions
4. Answer patient questions
5. Document education provided

### 4.5 Care Coordination

#### Handoff Documentation
1. Document shift handoffs
2. Update patient status and care plans
3. Communicate with other care team members
4. Ensure continuity of care

#### Discharge Preparation
1. Review discharge instructions
2. Coordinate follow-up appointments
3. Provide patient education materials
4. Arrange transportation if needed
5. Document discharge teaching

## 5. Doctor Guide

### 5.1 Consultation Workflow

#### Starting a Consultation
1. Review your patient queue
2. Click on the next patient
3. Begin the 5-step consultation process

#### Step 1: Patient Overview Hub
1. Review patient demographics and history
2. Check recent vital signs
3. Review allergies and current medications
4. Note any alerts or concerns

#### Step 2: Clinical Assessment Center
1. Document chief complaint
2. Record symptoms with severity levels
3. Perform physical examination by systems
4. Document findings

#### Step 3: Treatment Plan Hub
1. Search and select ICD-10 diagnoses
2. Develop treatment plan
3. Consider clinical guidelines
4. Document rationale

#### Step 4: Final Review Station
1. Order prescriptions with safety checks
2. Order laboratory tests if needed
3. Schedule follow-up appointments
4. Review treatment plan

#### Step 5: Summary & Handoff Dashboard
1. Review complete consultation
2. Generate AI-assisted summary
3. Handoff to pharmacy and laboratory
4. Complete consultation

### 5.2 Clinical Decision Support

#### Drug Interaction Checking
1. When prescribing medications, the system automatically:
   - Checks for drug-drug interactions
   - Verifies allergy contraindications
   - Reviews dosing appropriateness
   - Flags high-risk medications

#### Clinical Guidelines
1. Access integrated clinical guidelines
2. Review evidence-based recommendations
3. Document adherence to guidelines
4. Access drug reference information

### 5.3 Test Ordering

#### Laboratory Orders
1. Click **"Order Labs"** in consultation
2. Search for tests by name or code
3. Select appropriate tests
4. Set priority (routine, urgent, stat)
5. Add clinical notes

#### Radiology Orders
1. Access imaging order module
2. Select imaging type and body part
3. Specify clinical indication
4. Set priority and special instructions

### 5.4 Prescription Management

#### Electronic Prescribing
1. Click **"Prescribe"** in consultation
2. Search for medications
3. Select strength, form, and quantity
4. Specify dosage instructions
5. Set number of refills
6. Review safety alerts

#### Prescription Monitoring
1. View prescription history
2. Monitor for potential abuse
3. Track medication adherence
4. Coordinate with pharmacists

### 5.5 Documentation

#### Clinical Notes
1. Use structured templates for common visits
2. Document SOAP notes (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan)
3. Include relevant clinical findings
4. Document patient education provided

#### Voice-to-Text Documentation (Future Feature)
1. Click microphone icon to start voice recording
2. Dictate clinical notes
3. System transcribes speech to text
4. Review and edit transcription

## 9. Troubleshooting

### 9.1 Common Issues

#### Login Problems
**Issue**: Can't log in to the system
**Solutions**:
1. Check if Caps Lock is on
2. Verify email and password
3. Try password reset if forgotten
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact IT support if issues persist

#### Slow Performance
**Issue**: System is running slowly
**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Close unnecessary browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Restart browser
5. Try accessing during off-peak hours
6. Contact support for persistent issues

#### Data Not Saving
**Issue**: Changes not being saved
**Solutions**:
1. Check for validation errors (red fields)
2. Ensure all required fields are completed
3. Try saving again
4. Check internet connection
5. Refresh the page and try again
6. Contact support if data loss occurs

### 9.2 Error Messages

#### "Session Expired"
- Your login session has timed out for security
- Save your work and log in again
- Consider enabling "Remember Me" for longer sessions

#### "Access Denied"
- You don't have permission for this action
- Contact your supervisor or administrator
- Verify you're logged in with the correct account

#### "Network Error"
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact IT support if connection issues persist

### 9.3 Getting Help

#### In-System Help
1. Click the **"?"** icon in the top navigation
2. Search for topics or browse categories
3. Access video tutorials and guides

#### Contacting Support
1. Click **"Support"** in the help menu
2. Describe your issue clearly
3. Include screenshots if possible
4. Provide your contact information
5. Response time: Within 4 hours for urgent issues

#### Emergency Support
- **System Down**: Call IT emergency line
- **Patient Safety Issue**: Contact clinical leadership immediately
- **Data Breach Concern**: Follow incident response protocol

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions

### 10.1 General Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Click your profile picture > "Profile Settings" > "Change Password"

**Q: Can I access the system from my phone?**
A: Yes, the system is mobile-responsive. Use your mobile browser to access the same URL.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Use the "Report Issue" link in the help menu or contact support.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and complies with HIPAA regulations.

### 10.2 Patient Portal Questions

**Q: How do I schedule an appointment?**
A: Log in to the patient portal and click "Schedule Appointment"

**Q: Can I view my lab results online?**
A: Yes, go to "Medical Records" > "Lab Results" in your patient portal

**Q: How do I request a prescription refill?**
A: Go to "Medications" and click "Request Refill" for any eligible medication

### 10.3 Clinical Staff Questions

**Q: How do I document a consultation?**
A: Start a consultation from your patient queue and follow the 5-step process

**Q: What if I find a drug interaction?**
A: The system will alert you automatically. Review the interaction details and choose an alternative if needed.

**Q: How do I order lab tests?**
A: In Step 4 of the consultation, click "Order Labs" and select the required tests

### 10.4 Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest 2 versions each)

**Q: Do I need special software?**
A: No, the system runs entirely in your web browser

**Q: Can I use the system offline?**
A: Limited offline functionality is available for some features, but full functionality requires internet connection

**Q: How do I export data?**
A: Most data tables have export options (PDF, Excel, CSV) in the top-right corner
